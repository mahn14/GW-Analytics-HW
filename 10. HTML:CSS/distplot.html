SalePrice Distribution

One of the most immediate steps when trying to predict the SalePrice variable is... to look at SalePrice!

Given that linear regression models work better when the variables are Normally distributed, seeing that the SalePrice variable comes with a pretty significant right skew should raise some concerns. Fortunately, a quick fix is applying a log transformation to the variable itself. Since interpretability of the model is not much a concern for us, this should be fine. On another note, it is important to remember that we are now predicting log(SalePrice) not SalePrice.
